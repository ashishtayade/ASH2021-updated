Sickle Cell Disease Phase 1 (NIH CRADA) Study Design

STATUS: CORE STUDY COMPLETED
EXTENSION ONGOING

A phase 1, non-randomized, open-lable dose escalation study of mitapivat in adults with sickle cell disease

  • Patients who participated in the core study, and who met eligibility criteria, were given the option of participating in a 48-week extension study.

Key Inclusion Criteria

  • Adults (age ≥18 years) with stable Hb SS disease
  • No transfusions or changes in hydroxyurea/L-glutamine within 90 days

Primary Endpoint

  • Safety and tolerability as assessed by frequency and severity of AEs
  • Changes in Hb and hemolytic markers

Secondary Endpoints

  • Pharmacokinetics
  • 2,3-DPG and ATP levels
  • O2 dissociation and sickling tendency

AEs = adverse events; BID = twice daily; CRADA = Cooperative Research and Development Agreement.

For additional details on the core study, search ClinicalTrials.gov for NCT04000165.
For additional details on the extension study, search ClinicalTrials.gov for NCT04610866.
