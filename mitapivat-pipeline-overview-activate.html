Study Design

STATUS: CORE STUDY COMPLETED
EXTENSION ONGOING

A phase 3, randomized, placebo-controlled, multicenter study of mitapivat in adult patients with pyruvate kinase (PK) deficiency who are not regularly transfuseda

Primary Efficacy Endpoint
Proportion of patients who achieve at least a 1.5 g/dL increase in hemoglobin sustained over multiple visits in the Optimized Dose Period.

Patient Population

  • Not regularly transfuseda adults
  • Baseline hemoglobin of ≤10.0 grams per deciliter
  • Excludes patients with 2 non-missense mutations, without the presence of another missense mutation, or those homozygous for the R479H mutation

Dose

  • Dose titration up to optimal hemoglobin response (5, 20 or 50 mg twice daily)

Secondary Endpoints

  • Hemoglobin response
  • Safety
  • Patient-reported outcomes (PRO)
EFFICACY

a≤4 transfusion episodes in the 12-month period up to the first day of study treatment, and no transfusions in the 3 months prior to the first dose.
BID = twice daily.
