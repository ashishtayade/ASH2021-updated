Study Design

STATUS: CORE STUDY COMPLETED
EXTENSION ONGOING

A phase 3, open-label, multicenter study of mitapivat in patients with pyruvate kinase (PK) deficiency who receive regular blood transfusionsa

Primary Efficacy Endpoint
Reduction in transfusion burdenb during the Optimized Dose Period over a 6 month period compared to the patient’s transfusion history.

Patient Population

  • Regularly transfuseda adults
  • Excludes patients with 2 non-missense mutations, without the presence of another missense mutation, or those homozygous for the R479H mutation

Dose

  • Dose titration to maximize effect (5, 20, or 50 mg twice daily)

Secondary and Exploratory Endpoints

  • Reduction in transfusion burden
  • Safety
  • Patient-reported outcomes (PRO)
EFFICACY

a≥6 transfusion episodes in the past 52 weeks.
b≥33% reduction in the number of red blood cell units transfused.
BID = twice daily.
